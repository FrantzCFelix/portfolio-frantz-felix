About Me

Hi there, my name is Frantz Felix. I'm glad you found my page! I'm originally from NJ and moved to Seattle just over a year ago. I have been reconnecting with family, making new friends, and going on new adventures! I got interested in tech at a young age, primarily from playing video games as a kid ! The way everything was rendered seemed like magic and I had to be apart of that magic. I first learned a little bit of BASIC in middle school for a “show and tell” then didn’t really jumpstart my coding experience until my freshman year of college where I learned C at IUPUI! Unfortunately Indian didn’t treat me too well, so I went back to NJ and learned C++ at Burlington County College and then transferred to Rutgers-Camden where I continued using C, being introduced to the linux operating system, taking other various Computer Science classes and even getting my Minor in animation!

When I’m not at my computer I enjoy getting outdoors music festivals, hiking, backpacking, and camping are my go-tos! I also enjoy indoor bouldering, I have only been doing it for a year but have gotten rather good at it! If i'm not being active I’m hanging out with friends, 3D modeling, 3D printing, or playing video games! I am currently addicted to Super Smash Brothers! Thanks for taking the time to visit my page and read my bio. I hope you like what you see!
